Foundations

Research & References

BSCRI is grounded in international supply chain frameworks, national development priorities, and regional trade agreements. This page outlines the key references that inform the methodology.

Frameworks & Standards

BSCRI draws on internationally recognized supply chain frameworks and quality standards.

Framework

SCOR Model

Supply Chain Operations Reference model — the industry standard for supply chain process management and performance measurement.

Framework

GSCF Framework

Global Supply Chain Forum framework — defines key supply chain processes and cross-functional integration.

Standard

ISO 20400

International standard for sustainable procurement — guides ethical and socially responsible sourcing practices.

Trade Agreement

WTO Trade Facilitation Agreement

International agreement to simplify customs procedures and improve cross-border trade efficiency.

Policy & Development

BSCRI aligns with Botswana's national development priorities and regional trade frameworks.

Trade Agreement

AfCFTA Agreement

African Continental Free Trade Area — the largest free trade area in the world, creating unprecedented opportunities for Botswana.

Policy

NDP 11

Botswana National Development Plan 11 — the government's blueprint for economic diversification and sustainable development.

Trade Agreement

SADC Trade Protocol

Southern African Development Community — regional integration framework for trade and economic cooperation.

Policy

Economic Diversification Drive

Botswana's strategic initiative to reduce dependency on diamonds and build a resilient, diversified economy.
