About BSCRI

Advancing Botswana's Supply Chain Competitiveness

The Botswana Supply Chain Readiness Index (BSCRI) is a national assessment platform developed by Supply Chain Circle (SCC) to measure and strengthen organizational readiness for regional and international trade.

What is BSCRI?

BSCRI is a professional assessment framework designed to evaluate an organization's supply chain capabilities across 7 critical dimensions.

The platform provides a comprehensive readiness score, maturity level classification, and a personalized action plan with practical recommendations. It is built specifically for the Botswana context, aligned with the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) and Botswana's National Development Plan 11 (NDP 11).

BSCRI serves as a strategic asset for businesses, policymakers, and development agencies seeking to build resilient, competitive supply chains.

7 Dimensions

Procurement, Logistics, Compliance, Digital, Supplier, Resilience, Trade

Our Story

Supply Chain Circle (SCC) is a Botswana-based community of supply chain professionals, academics, and practitioners.

We are dedicated to advancing supply chain management, procurement, logistics, trade readiness, and digital transformation within Botswana and the wider African region. Our work is driven by a belief that strong supply chains are the foundation of economic growth and resilience.

BSCRI is our flagship initiative — a contribution to building a data-driven, competitive, and inclusive supply chain ecosystem for Botswana.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with intelligence and tools to build world-class supply chains.
