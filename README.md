# Botswana Supply Chain Readiness Index (BSCRI)

**A professional assessment platform for measuring supply chain competitiveness in Botswana.**

---

## Overview

The Botswana Supply Chain Readiness Index (BSCRI) is a comprehensive assessment tool designed to measure an organization's capability to compete effectively in regional and international supply chains. Developed specifically for the Botswana context, BSCRI helps organizations understand their supply chain strengths, identify gaps, and receive actionable recommendations for improvement.

### Key Features

- **25-Question Assessment** — Covers 7 dimensions of supply chain readiness
- **Real-Time Scoring** — Instant calculation of dimension and overall scores
- **Maturity Levels** — Foundational → Emerging → Developing → Established → Advanced
- **Actionable Recommendations** — Personalized improvement plan with Botswana-specific resources
- **Admin Dashboard** — View, filter, analyze, and export all assessment data
- **Professional Design** — Enterprise-grade UI suitable for government, business, and academic stakeholders

### The 7 Dimensions

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Procurement Capability | Strategic sourcing, contract management, supplier evaluation |
| Logistics & Operations | Transportation, warehousing, inventory, last-mile delivery |
| Compliance & Governance | Regulatory compliance, procurement policy, governance structures |
| Digital Capability | Technology adoption, data management, digital transformation |
| Supplier & Partnership Ecosystem | Supplier relationships, local content, collaboration |
| Resilience & Risk | Disruption readiness, business continuity, risk management |
| Trade & Growth Readiness | Export capability, cross-border logistics, regional market knowledge |
